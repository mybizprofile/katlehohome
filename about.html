Katleho Home for the Disabled

Katleho Home For the Disabled was established in 1999 from an initiative by: Ms Maputle Kentse Dora Ntsoele

For almost 22 years, Katleho Home for the Disabled centre has successfully used the law to establish, protect and affirm children's rights. The centre therefore has empowered the disability sector for the benefit of children with disablities in the community of Evaton, South Africa.

The centre has an operational programme focusing on children with disabilities, a system that is set to protect and realize their rights, a home indeed for care.
