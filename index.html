Caring for Every Child, Every Day

A safe haven for children with disabilities to thrive and grow.

Katleho Home For the Disabled established in 1999

Katleho Home For the Disabled was established in 1999 from an initiative by: Ms Maputle Kentse Dora Ntsoele.

For almost 22 years, Katleho Home for the Disabled centre has successfully used the law to establish, protect and affirm children's rights. The centre therefore has empowered the disability sector for the benefit of children with disabilities in the community of Evaton, South Africa.

The centre has an operational programme focusing on children with disabilities, a system that is set to protect and realize their rights, a home indeed for care.
